===== Guinea-Bissau Information =====

Guinea-Bissau, officially the Republic of Guinea-Bissau, is a country in West Africa that covers 36,125 square kilometres (13,948 sq mi) with an estimated population of 1,726,000. It borders [[country:senegal:home|Senegal]] to the north and [[country:guinea:home|Guinea]] to the south-east.

For more information, please visit [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guinea-Bissau|Wikipedia]].
