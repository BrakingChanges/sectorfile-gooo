===== Sierra Leone Information =====

Sierra Leone, officially the Republic of Sierra Leone, is a country on the southwest coast of West Africa. It is bordered by [[liberia|Liberia]] to the southeast and [[guinea|Guinea]] surrounds the northern half of the nation. Covering a total area of 71,740 km2 (27,699 sq mi), Sierra Leone has a tropical climate, with diverse environments ranging from savanna to rainforests. The country has a population of 7,092,113 as of the 2015 census. Freetown is the capital and largest city. The country is divided into five administrative regions, which are subdivided into 16 districts.

For more information, please visit [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sierra_Leone|Wikipedia]].
