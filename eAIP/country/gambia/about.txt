===== The Gambia Information =====

The Gambia, officially the Republic of The Gambia, is a country in West Africa. Geographically, The Gambia is the smallest country in continental Africa. It is surrounded by [[country:senegal:home|Senegal]] on all sides except for the western part, which is bordered by the Atlantic Ocean. 

For more information, please visit [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Gambia|Wikipedia]].
