===== Guinea Republic Information =====

Guinea, officially the Republic of Guinea, is a coastal country in West Africa. It borders the Atlantic Ocean to the west, [[country:guinea-bissau:home|Guinea-Bissau]] to the northwest, [[country:senegal:home|Senegal]] to the north, [[country:mali:home|Mali]] to the northeast, [[country:ivory-coast:home|Cote d'Ivoire]] to the southeast, and [[country:sierra-leone:home|Sierra Leone]] and [[country:liberia:home|Liberia]] to the south. It is sometimes referred to as Guinea-Conakry after its capital Conakry, to distinguish it from other territories in the eponymous region such as Guinea-Bissau and [[country:equatorial-guinea:home|Equatorial Guinea]]. It has a population of 13.5 million and an area of 245,857 square kilometres (94,926 sq mi). 

For more information, please visit [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Guinea|Wikipedia]].
