===== Dakar Continental Information =====

Dakar Continental FIR is a Flight Information Region that spans multiple countries, namely [[country:senegal:home|Senegal]] (in full), [[country:mauritania:home|Mauritania]] (in full), [[country:ivory-coast:home|Ivory Coast]] (in full), [[country:guinea-bissau:home|Guinea-Bissau]] (partially), [[country:mali:home|Mali]] (partially) and a slice of airspace from Burkina Faso.

[[airspace:goooo:home|Dakar Oceanic]] is part of the Dakar FIR, but operational purposes, we've split it from Dakar Continental.
